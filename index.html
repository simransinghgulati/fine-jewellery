About Us

Why should Gold Jewellery Be Boring?

Twist79 is a contemporary fine jewellery brand based in United Arab Emirates.

Twist denotes a change and 79 is the atomic number of Gold, hence it stands for redefining gold jewellery and bringing about contemporary fashion forward aesthetics.

The DNA of Twist79 jewellery is to offer an unconventional take on the usual silhouettes, contemprorary designs and a unique perspective on - Art Deco and Modern Art inspirations. We offer subtle yet adventurous products for those with an aspiration for both style and quality.

Born from the love of runway fashion, young spirit and Middle East’s top-notch extravagance, Twist79 jewellery is manufactured globally using best manufacturing techniques from Italy, Hong Kong, Vietnam and Japan in 18-karat gold. Our products are preppy, edgy, modern, outrageous and bold perfect for - the everyday office confidence, a look at me lunch with your besties or a special glamour night out.

Our products are sold all around the world to businesses which appreciate their quality and our reliability and technical expertise.

Our Services

What we Offer

Trust & Compliance

One of the main goals of our company policy is to guarantee weight, feature, finish and compliance. We carry out systematic controls on our products with very efficient high-precision instruments. Every single step of our production is monitored and the metal title is guaranteed.

Timely delivery

We adhere delivery time lines ut-most sincerely . We believe and value your time, with on the mark deliveries without fail.

Original Designs

With a team of in-house experienced designers, we value creativity and deliver originality in products.

Product Customization

We provide maximum customization as we create our designs from concept to creation. This enables our products to be unique, exclusive and recognizable in the market.

Wide Product Portfolio

With our expertise and technical knowhow we constantly strive to experiment and expand with new product categories. Enabling us to offer our client a wide range of products.

Prompt Tracking

Tracking and following up your order is just an email away at every stage. With our efficient product team, we cumulate, track, process and fetch you previous orders seamlessly.

Fashion Forward Products

Following customers changing needs closely and monitoring international trends, we strive to offer products which are utmost fashion forward. Conducting business with us assures you are at par with the ongoing trends.

Our Vision and Responsibility

We believe in the capacity and strength of women workforce in the jewellery industry, with an aim to maintain more than 60% women employees at every time. We stand for offering pieces by the woman - for the women, and trust such pieces have a power and charm which is un-comparable.

We promise at every stage to be sustainable in every sense of the word, based on respect towards workers, product safety, transparancy regarding the production cycle and the highest possible reduction of damage to the environment.
