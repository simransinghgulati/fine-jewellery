Why should gold jewellery be boring?

Twist79 is a contemporary fine jewellery label rooted in United Arab Emirates.

Twist stands for a change and 79 is the atomic number of Gold, hence it stands for redefining gold jewellery and bringing about contemporary fashion forward aesthetics.

The essence of Twist79 jewellery is to offer unconventional take on the usual silhouettes, characteristic designs and a unique perspective on Art Deco and modern art inspiration. We offers subtle yet adventurous pieces for those with an aspiration for both style and quality.
